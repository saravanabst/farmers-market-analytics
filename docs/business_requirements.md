# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

Farmers Market Analytics Platform

---

## Project Overview

FarmFresh Market is an online marketplace that connects customers with local farmers and vendors. The business generates sales data daily and requires an analytics platform to support operational and strategic decision-making.

The objective of this project is to build a production-grade analytics solution that transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Objective

Develop a centralized reporting system that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance through reliable metrics, automated reports, and interactive dashboards.

---

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder        | Business Need                                |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| CEO                | Monitor business growth and revenue trends   |
| Sales Manager      | Identify top-selling products and categories |
| Operations Manager | Evaluate vendor performance                  |
| Finance Team       | Track revenue and profitability              |
| Inventory Manager  | Monitor product demand and stock movement    |
| Marketing Team     | Understand customer purchasing trends        |

---

## Business Problems

* Sales data is scattered across multiple sources.
* Reports are generated manually.
* Business decisions are delayed due to lack of timely insights.
* No centralized dashboard exists for management.

---

## Project Scope

The analytics platform will:

* Store transactional data in MySQL.
* Perform business analysis using SQL.
* Automate reporting with Python and Pandas.
* Generate Excel reports.
* Provide interactive Tableau dashboards.
* Produce business insights for decision-makers.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Top Selling Products
* Top Vendors
* Sales by Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* City-wise Sales

---

## Expected Deliverables

* MySQL Database
* SQL Scripts
* Python Automation
* Excel Reports
* Tableau Dashboard
* Business Documentation
* GitHub Repository

---

## Assumptions

* Sales data is available in a structured format.
* Product, vendor, and customer records are complete.
* Reports are refreshed on a scheduled basis.
* Stakeholders use Tableau dashboards for business reporting.
